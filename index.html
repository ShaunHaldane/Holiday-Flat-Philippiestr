Disabled Friendly Holiday Appartment in Haltern am See.


We have a 2 bedroom appartment with kitchen, livingroom, bathroom and hallway to rent for short or long stay. The appartment is easily accessible from our outside elevator. We are fully equiped to suit the needs of our disabled guests and believe that practicality is the key to a comfortable stay. We are just a 10 minute walk from the Haltern train station and a 10 minute walk from the Haltern town center.
